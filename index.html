Curious Minds Ask

What is Poetry?

Poetry is the heartbeat of language, the soul of expression, and the art of painting with words.

Why Read Poetry?

Reading poetry is like sipping on the elixir of life, it nourishes the mind, stirs the heart, and ignites the imagination.

How to Write Poems?

To write poetry, you must dance with words, court the muse, and let your soul spill ink onto the page.

Is Poetry Dead?

Poetry isn't dead; it's a phoenix rising from the ashes of indifference, breathing life into the mundane.

Poetry or Prose?

Poetry is the rebel yell of the soul, the wild child of literature, while prose is the steady heartbeat of storytelling.

In every poem, a universe unfolds, in every verse, a story untold.

" The Vulnerable Me "

Your glittering eyes,

The beauty behind those smiles.

Your weak personality,

And especially your short height.

They all do matter to me,

As I admire you, full of sincerity.

Although my feelings are completely in decision,

I still have two... Two valid options.

As I don't have enough confidence and courage,

My heart is locked on for this feelings like it's on a cage.

My standards is far from your expectations,

That's why while it's early, I'm controlling my emotions.

I may not have completely known you,

But I know deep inside me, my feelings do.

I don't understand whether your actually what I used to see,

Or will just be another part of a memory.

We may have gotten some eye contact,

But I can't explain, either those are with feelings that I cannot interact.

Or I'm just being me, waiting for the right time to act.

While there is time going on a clock.

I found myself... stuck.
